Ingredients:
 - 1 kg leek
 - 1/2 kg minced meat
 - 200 g cheese spread
 - 1 l broth
 - Oil
 - spices (salt, pepper, ...)
 - paprika powder

Directions:
  1. Cut the leek in small slices
  2. Heaten oil in a big pot and roast the meat + Salt, Pepper, paprika
     powder or whatever spices you want to.
  3. Sauté briefly the leek with the meet and then add the broth. Bring
     everything to a boil and simmer for about 15 min
  4. Now turn off oven or gas and add the cheese spread. Stir it and
     get all the cheese melted... Voilà :)
